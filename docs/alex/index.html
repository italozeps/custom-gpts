Alex · Delegated Understanding

Prototype landing page for the Alex and Alex++ concept — transforming how LLMs assist in reading, reflection, and action.

Orientation

Alex is an LLM role dedicated not to conversation but to delegated understanding — helping humans read, interpret, and act through structured meaning. While a typical LLM responds to a query, Alex processes a text, building a semantic bridge toward comprehension and reflection.

Alex++

Alex++ extends this idea to a collaborative environment, where a “Boss” LLM coordinates multiple “Crew” LLMs. They automate the generation of Alex pages and interactive reading tools from curated datasets or text collections.
