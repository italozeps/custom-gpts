John von Neumann might’ve been the most brilliant human who ever lived.
Here’s what just one mind contributed:
  • Mathematics: von Neumann universe, ordinal numbers, functional analysis, ergodic theory, measure theory.
  • Computer Science: von Neumann architecture, stored program concept, EDVAC report, numerical analysis, self-replicating automata.
  • Physics: Mathematical foundations of quantum mechanics, Hilbert space formalism, von Neumann entropy, quantum logic, Manhattan Project, hydrodynamics, explosion theory.
  • Economics & Game Theory: Founded game theory, co-authored Theory of Games and Economic Behavior, minimax theorem, expected utility theory, equilibrium theory.
  • Logic & Philosophy: Mathematical logic, proof theory, axiomatization of set theory, anticipated AI and machine cognition.
  • Engineering & Applied Science: Aerodynamics, fluid dynamics simulation, nuclear and thermonuclear weapons, automation advocate.
AI will soon give us billions of John von Neumanns.
